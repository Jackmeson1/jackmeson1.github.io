## Overview

This personal site tracks only the minimal data required to understand readership and keep content running smoothly. No personal information is sold or shared with third parties.

## Analytics

The site uses privacy-friendly analytics tools to monitor aggregate traffic patterns such as page views, referrers, and device types. Individual user journeys are not tracked.

## Cookies

No first-party cookies are set beyond those required by the analytics provider. If embedded services (such as video players or forms) introduce additional cookies, they will disclose their own policies at the point of interaction.
